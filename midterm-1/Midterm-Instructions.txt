Exam Instructions:

1. Leave Login.jar running throughout the midterm
2. Open the PDF file to see the midterm questions
3. Work on the files within the exam folder - do not move them elsewhere
4. When you are finished or out of time, double-check that you have saved all of your files and that they contain the correct information
5. Close Login.jar, this will upload your files automatically
6. Verify your upload contents by looking in the 'archives' folder for a firstname-lastname.zip file. This zip file should have all of your files
7. Sign out at the front of the room before leaving

If you think there has been an error with the system at any point, ask one of the proctors for assistance.
